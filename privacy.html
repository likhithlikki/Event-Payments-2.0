🛡️ Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information

📅 Last Updated: December 2024 · Effective Date: December 2024

📋 Table of Contents

🔒 Our Core Privacy Promise

Your payment details are NEVER shown publicly. Phone numbers, UTR transaction IDs, full addresses, and payment amounts are visible only to authorized event administrators. The public donor list shows only first name and contribution amount.
✅ Phone Hidden ✅ UTR Hidden ✅ Email Hidden ✅ Full Amount Admin-Only

📝 1. Information We Collect

When you make a contribution through EventPay, we collect:

  • Full Name — Used to identify your contribution
  • Village / Area — Used for organizing donor lists by location
  • Phone Number — Used to prevent duplicate contributions and for admin verification only
  • Contribution Amount — The amount you contributed
  • UTR / Transaction Reference — Your UPI transaction ID for payment verification
  • Submission Date & Time — Auto-recorded at time of submission

When you file a complaint, we additionally collect:

  • Email address (optional, for reply)
  • Complaint description
  • Any attached screenshots (stored securely)

🎯 2. How We Use Your Information

  • To record and verify your payment contribution
  • To display your name on the public donor list (if enabled by the organizer)
  • To prevent duplicate or fraudulent payments
  • To allow admins to verify UPI transaction authenticity
  • To send you a payment receipt (via the app, not email)
  • To respond to complaints or support requests
  • To generate anonymized analytics (village-wise totals, contribution counts)

We do NOT use your data for marketing, advertising, or selling to third parties.

💳 3. Payment Data Protection

EventPay operates on UPI — we do not process, store, or have access to your bank account credentials, debit/credit card numbers, or UPI PIN.

The UTR (Unique Transaction Reference) you submit is:

  • Stored securely in a private Google Sheet accessible only to event admins
  • Used only for verifying that your UPI payment was made
  • Never shown on any public page
  • Checked against a fraud detection algorithm to prevent fake submissions
⚠️ Important: If you receive a suspicious call asking for your UPI PIN, OTP, or bank details claiming to be from EventPay — it is a scam. EventPay never asks for your PIN, OTP, or password.

🚫 4. What We Do NOT Share Publicly

The following information is never visible to the public:

  • Your phone number
  • Your UTR / transaction ID
  • Your email address
  • Your exact contribution amount (on certain configurations)
  • Complaint details or correspondence
  • Admin-flagged payment status or fraud scores

The public donor list (if enabled) shows only:

  • Your name (as entered)
  • Your contribution amount
  • Date of contribution

If you wish to be hidden from the public donor list, please contact the event organizer or contact us.

🔐 5. Data Storage & Security

All data is stored in Google Sheets, which is:

  • Protected by Google's enterprise-grade security
  • Accessible only to authorized admins with a username and password
  • Subject to session-based authentication with automatic expiry
  • Backed up automatically by Google

Admin actions are logged in an audit trail. Every change — status updates, data edits, logins — is recorded with timestamp and admin name.

We take reasonable precautions to protect your data but cannot guarantee absolute security of any internet transmission.

✊ 6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access — Request a copy of your personal data we hold
  • Correction — Request correction of incorrect information
  • Deletion — Request deletion of your data (subject to event organizer discretion for financial records)
  • Visibility Control — Request to be hidden from the public donor list

To exercise these rights, contact us or submit a request. We will respond within 7 business days.

🍪 7. Cookies & Local Storage

EventPay uses browser localStorage (not cookies) for:

  • Theme preference (dark/light mode)
  • Admin session token (expires automatically)

We do not use tracking cookies, advertising pixels, or analytics that identify you personally. No data from localStorage is transmitted to any third party.

🔗 8. Third-Party Services

EventPay uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Apps Script & Google Sheets — For backend data processing. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Fonts — For typography (Sora, JetBrains Mono). No personal data shared.
  • UPI Payment Apps (PhonePe, GPay, Paytm, BHIM) — Payment is processed directly between you and the app. EventPay does not receive your financial credentials.

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party services.

👶 9. Children's Privacy

EventPay is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has submitted information through our platform, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.
