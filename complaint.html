❓ Common Questions

Payments are confirmed via Razorpay. If deducted but not confirmed, please wait 2–4 hours. If still not confirmed, submit a complaint with your Razorpay Payment ID.
Refunds are processed by the event organizer. Submit a refund request with your receipt number and reason. Refunds take 5–7 business days.
Use the "Check Status" section on the event page with your phone number. You can download your receipt from there. If email receipt is missing, check your spam folder.
Event codes are 8–10 characters (e.g. WED25001). Make sure there are no spaces. If still not working, contact the organizer or submit a complaint here.
