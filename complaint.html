❓ Common Questions

Payments via UPI are confirmed within minutes. If deducted but not confirmed, wait 2–4 hours. If still not confirmed, submit a complaint with your UTR number.
Refunds are processed by the event organizer. Submit a refund request with your UTR and reason. Refunds take 5–7 business days once approved.
Use the "Check Status" page with your phone number or UTR to verify your payment. Your record appears immediately after successful payment.
Submit a complaint with your UTR number and the correct information. Our admin will update your record within 24 hours.
